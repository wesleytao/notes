This should be decided by Week 20 at the latest (registration deadlines, drug testing protocols, and show calendars vary).

| Factor | NPC | Natural (WNBF, OCB, NANBF, etc.) |
|--------|-----|-----------------------------------|
| Drug testing | Polygraph only (if tested at all) | Urinalysis + polygraph. Some require 7-year natural history. |
| Competition | Larger fields, more competitors | Smaller fields, more even playing field |
| Stage conditioning | Judges may expect harder conditioning | More reasonable conditioning expectations |
| Creatine | Allowed | Allowed in most federations (verify) |
| Show availability | More frequent, more locations | Fewer shows, may require travel |

> If you are lifetime natural and your goal is fair competition, a tested natural federation is likely the better experience. If you want the largest stage and don't mind potentially competing against enhanced athletes, NPC gives you more options.
